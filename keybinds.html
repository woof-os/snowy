Keybinds For Woof OS (Qtile)

Launching and Closing Programs

Launch Kitty
Super Return

Launch The Rofi Run Launcher
Super R

Close Active Window
Super Q

Moving Focus

Move Focus Left
Super H

Move Focus Right
Super L

Move Focus Up
Super J

Move Focus Down
Super K

Move Focus To Other Windows
Super Space

Moving Windows

Move Window Left
Super Shift H

Move Window Right
Super Shift L

Move Window Up
Super Shift J

Move Window Down
Super Shift K

Other

Launch Brave (Web Browser)
Super B

Launch Pcmanfm (File Manager)
Super C

Launch Flameshot (Screenshot tool)
Super F

Other keybinds can be found and configured in the config file, ~/.config/qtile/config.py by default.
